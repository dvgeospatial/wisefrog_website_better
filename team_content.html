Wise Frog is a communications agency and consultancy based in Norfolk, UK.

Our Services

  • Publications development from conception to delivery, including full editorial and print management
  • Media relations, campaign strategy and evaluation; interview training
  • Guidance on EU advocacy and access to EU funding
  • Digital mapping and 3D visualisations

The Team

Katharine Mill is a journalist, editor and communications manager with over 25 years' experience, including 10 in Brussels. She has advised public authorities and non-profit groups on EU affairs and communication strategies, particularly in the fields of energy and environment policy. She is also the author of several travel guides to Bruges and Brussels, and continues to write articles for general media on a variety of topics.

Daniel Voisey worked for 20 years as an archeologist, and is expert in providing GIS digital mapping services, as well as 3D visualisations for the purposes of archeological, planning and building projects.
